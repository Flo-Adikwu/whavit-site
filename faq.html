FAQS

I want to request a Whavit professional:
Did you find a WhavPro that you liked? Great! You can favourite the Pro. You can favourite up to 3 pros and get to pick reschedule with them directly in the app for your next cleaning subject to their availability.
What happens when my WhavPro never arrived?
Please use the chat button to get in touch with our customer experience team; we aim to respond to urgent issues like this within minutes. If you'd like a replacement booking 24 hours from now, please include this request along with your availability. If you'd like a replacement booking but aren't sure when you're available, just let us know!
Can I request a refund?
Yes, you can if the service is not satisfactory. We'll automatically credit your account if you cancel a booking that's already been billed. For unsatisfactory service, the WhavPro will come back to do a fresh round of cleaning or we get another WhavPro to do the cleaning if the first WhavPro is not available.
Is Whavit insured?
Trust, safety, security and Quality Assurance are important pillars of WhavIt, WhavPros are insured in the event that anything goes wrong while cleaning. If something is awry, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer experience team.
